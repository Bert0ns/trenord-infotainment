## Locked Page
- Screen you see when you open the app with no train info inserted. 
- You can only access settings.
- Lets you log in by manual insertion of the ticket code, and by inserting the destination station among the possible ones.
  - By logging in you get access to the rest of the app, starting from the home page.


## Home Page (dashboard)
Dashboard with relevant info:
- Train status, delays, crowding levels, etc.
- Weather of the destination at the time of arrival
- ETA, percent progress
- News related to the destination, with AI summary


## Journey Page
- Main: Detailed timeline of the train stops
- Google maps map that shows your position in real time, with the railways highlighted.


## Journey Destination Page
- Relevant AI summarized news of the destination
- Relevant Points of Interest near the destination
  - with a button that lets you get the direction to get there
- Connections to other public transport lines or services
- Weather state, air quality, UV


## Shake to report Page
- Shake the mobile device to pop-up this page
- Lets you report an issue 
  - Train is too crowded
  - Air conditioning not working
  - Dirty seats
  - Bugs in the app
  - etc


## Settings page
- Shake to report button
- Notifications management
  - Notifications related to jurney progress changes
    - Train delays, cancellations, etc.
    - Weather changes at the destination
    - About to arrive at the destination
- Language management (Italian and English)
- Toggle Accelerometer-based anti-sickness mode (e.g. for long train rides) (IOS style)

## Media Page
- Sponsored/partner media access (e.g. music, podcasts, etc.) during the journey
  - film, podcasts
