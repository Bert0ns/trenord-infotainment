## Home Page
Dashboard of sorts with relevant info:
- Train status, delays, crowding levels, etc.
- Weather of the destination at the time of arrival
- ETA, percent progress
- Map of the route with current location
- News related to the destination, with AI summary

## Features
- Notifications related to jurney progress changes
  - Train delays, cancellations, etc.
  - Weather changes at the destination
  - News related to the destination
  - About to arrive at the destination
- Shake to report an issue (e.g. train is too crowded, bugs in the app, etc.)
- Accelerometer-based anti-sickness mode (e.g. for long train rides)
- Sponsored/partner media access (e.g. music, podcasts, etc.) during the journey
- Points of interest near the destination, connections to other lines or services

## Project Constraints
- Test coverage for non-UI code
- Support for both Android and iOS platforms (Expo + React Native)
